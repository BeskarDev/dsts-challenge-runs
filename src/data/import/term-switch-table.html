 Digifarm DigiFarm Unifying the capitalization style. My thinking being, if it's Digi + a full word, the second word should be capitalized.
The official text is very inconsistent in this regard: there's DigiFish and Digimeat and so on. Some are even hyphenated.
In cases where it's "Digi" + part of another word, I didn't capitalize the partial word, like Digivice (device), Digimental (elemental).
